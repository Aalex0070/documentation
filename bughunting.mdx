> Join our Bug Hunting Program by identifying and reporting issues you encounter while using the bot. Not only will you help improve our services, but you'll also receive recognition for your efforts.

<Steps>
  <Step title="First Step">
    Use the bot actively in different scenarios.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Second Step">
    Report any bugs or issues you find with detailed descriptions and steps to reproduce.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Third Step">
    Upon verifying the bugs, you'll be able to collect a **Bug Hunter** role!
  </Step>
</Steps>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Tier 1 | Bug Hunter">
    **Challenge:** Find 2 bugs with the bot to earn a **special Bug Hunter role!**


  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Tier 2 | Bug Squasher">
    **Challenge:** Find 4 bugs with the bot to earn a **special Bug Squasher role!**
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Tier 3 | Bug Exterminator">
    **Challenge:** Find 4 bugs with the bot to earn a **special Bug Exterminator role!**
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
