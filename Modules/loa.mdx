> This feature aids in monitoring members on Leave of Absence (LOA) and automatically sends notifications upon the conclusion of their specified absence period.
---
<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Commands">
    - **/loa request** - Log a loa request.
    - **/loa active** - View all active loa's.
    - **/loa manage** - Manage a staff members loa.
