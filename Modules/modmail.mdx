---
title: Modmail
description: Manage communications with modmail
---

> Modmail allows you to directly message a server's staff team through the Astro Birb bot. You can easily choose the server you wish to contact for assistance or inquiries.

---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Commands">
    - **/modmail reply** - Reply to a modmaill.
    - **/modmail close** - Close a modmail conversation.
    - **/modmail snippets create** - Create a modmail snippet.
    - **/modmail snippets delete** - Delete a modmail snippet.
    - **/modmail snippets list** - View all the modmail snippets.


  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
