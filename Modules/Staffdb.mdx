title: Staff Panel
description: Have a public/private staff panel where you can view timezone, rank from a dropdown menu.
---


<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Introductions">
     > You can run /staff introduction where as a staff you can set your little introduction.

  </Tab>


    <Tab title="Timezone">
        > While running /staff add you can also add an option for what timezone the staff member is.

    </Tab>
